EMNLP 2015: Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing — September 17–21, 2015 — Lisboa, Portugal.

emnlp2015

SIGDAT, the Association for Computational Linguistics special interest group on linguistic data and corpus-based approaches to NLP, invites submissions to EMNLP 2015.

The conference will be held on September 17–21 (Thu–Mon) in Lisboa, Portugal.
