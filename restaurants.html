Restaurants

The organization of EMNLP 2015 has prepared a list of recommended restaurants. All of these restaurants have easy access to the conference venue, either by walking or by using the public transportation network.

The website for Turismo de Lisboa, a non-profit organization promoting Lisbon as a tourist destination and one the institutions supporting EMNLP 2015, has several suggestions for restaurants and for the local gastronomy of Lisbon, ranging from traditional and typical taverns to sophisticated and international sites.

The following map shows the locations for conference venue and for the different restaurants that are listed on this page.


An e-mail discussion group for EMNLP 2015 has also been created, with the aim of connecting people attending the conference (e.g., for helping students to arrange for accommodation on a shared basis, for supporting the exchange of tips on local restaurants and attractions, or for supporting general discussions about the conference). Fell free to join the discussion group.
