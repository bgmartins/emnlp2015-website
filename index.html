EMNLP 2015: Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing — October 25–29, 2014 — Lisboa, Portugal.

emnlp2015

SIGDAT, the Association for Computational Linguistics special interest group on linguistic data and corpus-based approaches to NLP, invites submissions to EMNLP 2015.

The conference will be held on October 25–29 (Sat–Wed) in Lisboa, Portugal.

Past EMNLP Conferences

SIGDAT started in 1993 with the series of Workshops on Very Large Corpora. WVLC turned into EMNLP in 1996, and has been held annually since then. Recent conferences included:

  • EMNLP 2014 Doha, Qatar
  • EMNLP 2013 Seattle, USA
  • EMNLP-CoNLL 2012 Jeju, Korea
  • EMNLP 2011 Edinburgh, Scotland
  • EMNLP 2010 Cambridge, USA
  • EMNLP 2009 Suntec, Singapore
  • EMNLP 2008 Waikiki, Hawaii, USA
  • EMNLP-CoNLL 2007 Prague, Czech Republic
  • EMNLP 2006 Sydney, Australia
  • HLT-EMNLP 2005 Vancouver, B.C., Canada
  • EMNLP 2004 Barcelona, Spain
